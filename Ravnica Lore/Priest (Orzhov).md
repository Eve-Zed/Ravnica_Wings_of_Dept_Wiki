Clerics, who aspire to serve an important function in the [[Orzhov Syndicate]], which continues to maintain the outward appearance of a religious institution, can serve as priests. Once they earn a position of respect in the syndicate, they will hear confessions and dole out penance (which is always of a financial nature). Their "calling" centres on collecting monetary tithes and offerings in a wide range of forms. Their spell casting ability comes from the collective power of the spirits that rule the guild, not from a god.
