The Theatre of Integrity of the [[Boros Legion]] is tasked to find the sleeper agents and hidden refuges of the [[House Dimir|Dimir]] and root any members of this subversive guild out.

A subdivision, the Dead Brigade, is tasked to neutralizes [[Spirit|spirits]].

It is led by [[Anksa|Lieutenant Anksa]] and operates out of [[Zelzo Base]].
