The Jelenn Column is one of Column of the [[Azorius Senate]]. It writes and enacts the laws of Ravnica, involving the efforts not just of the designated legislators like senators, but also a network of bureaucrats: aides, writers, researchers, lobbyists, clerks, and so on. The Jelenn Column is led by a [[Vedalken]] [[lawmage]] named [[Uzana]].
