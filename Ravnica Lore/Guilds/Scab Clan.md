Members of the Scab clan display scars and body modifications, which they view as expressions of the powerful rage they harbor within themselves. The clan has grown in influence by engulfing or destroying several smaller clans in recent years, but the leader of the Scabs, a corpulent [[Giant]] known as [[Narbulg Nine Fingers]], has not gone so far as to challenge the [[Burning Tree clan]].
