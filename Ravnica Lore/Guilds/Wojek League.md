The Wojek League is the law enforcement division of the [[Boros Legion]]. The "'Jeks" are charged with maintaining the peace and wellbeing of the people of the districts of the Ravnica City. The Wojek League are the elite soldiers. They serve the twin functions of military police and military intelligence. The current leader is [[Anksa|Commander Anksa]].
The headquarters of the Wojek League used to be the [[Parhelion]] but is now [[Zelzo Base]].
