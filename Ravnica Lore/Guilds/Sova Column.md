The Sova Column is one of the Columns in [[Azorius Senate]]. It consists of judges and the extensive legal apparatus surrounding them. It is tasked with adjudicating and arbitrating the effects of the law. The column employs legal aides, pages, librarians, experts on precedent, advocates for the accused, public prosecutors, and judges. The arbiter of the Sova Column is [[Leonos II]].
