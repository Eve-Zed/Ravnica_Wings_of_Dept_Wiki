[[Wojek League|Wojek]] Lieutenant Anksa of the [[Boros Legion]] is youngest ever to reach her rank and the leader of the [[theatre of Integrity]]. She operates out of the [[Zelzo Base]] and is tasked with end the criminal activities of [[House Dimir|Dimir]]. She has also appointed a special Group named the Dead Brigade which focus is neutralizing spirits especially the among the [[Orzhov Syndicate|Orzhov]].
