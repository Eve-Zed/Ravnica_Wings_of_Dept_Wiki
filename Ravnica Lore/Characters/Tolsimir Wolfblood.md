Tolsimir Wolfblood is an [[Elf|elven]] evangelist of the [[Selesnya Conclave]] and the leader of the [[Ledev Guardian|Ledev Guardians]].

Tolsimir is a great warrior and a skilled archer who wears green and silver armor. His mount is a massive white wolf named [[Voja Fenstalker]], the father of [[Biracazir]]. He is more dedicated to his guild than even his own life.
