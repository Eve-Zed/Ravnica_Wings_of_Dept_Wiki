Commander Grozdan is a [[Minotaur]] Soldier and operates out of the [[Kamen Fortress]]. He leads the [[Theatre of Order]] which is tasked to eliminate [[Cult of Rakdos|Rakdos]] assassins and spree killers. He is often describes as being very aggressive.
