Commander Yaszen is a [[Human]] Skynight who ascended quickly due to his bravery and strategic thinking, he is commander of the [[Horizon Military Academy]] and leader of the [[Theatre of Recruitment]], which is responsible for recruiting and training new members. He specialized on flying mounts breeding and training [[Roc|rocs]] and [[Griffin|griffins]].
