Sunhome, the Guildhall of the [[Boros Legion|Boros]], is a combined fortress, barracks, and place of worship. It is impregnable, serves as a symbol of military might, and is the base of operations for the [[Wojek League|League of Wojek]].

Sunhome is guarded by the veterans of the Sunhome Guard.
