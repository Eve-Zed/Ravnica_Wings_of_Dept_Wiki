# Combat
## Melee
- Cast [[Shadow Blade]] with [[Extended Spell]] to get [[Advantage]] on [[Concentration]] [[Saving Throw|saving throws]]
- Attack with [[True Strike]] using the [[Shadow Blade]]
- Use [[Innate Sorcery]] for [[Advantage]] with attack rolls
- Reroll Damage with [[Savage Attacker]] once per turn
- Reroll Damage with [[Empowered Spell]] with Sorcery Points
- Use [[Celestial Revelation]] for extra damage per turn and
	- [[Heavenly Wings]] [[Fly Speed]]
	- [[Inner Radiance]] Damage Aura (but cancels [[Dim Light]] [[Advantage]] of [[Shadow Blade]])
	- [[Necrotic Shroud]] Fear Aura gives enemies the [[Frightened]] condition
## Ranged
- Throw [[Shadow Blade]] with [[True Strike]]
	- Range 6m normal range and 18m with [[Disadvantage]] (can be cancelled with [[Restore Balance]]) 
	- Reroll Damage with [[Savage Attacker]] once per turn
- [[Sorcerous Burst]]  becomes stronger if you roll max damage
	- Good in combination with [[Empowered Spell]]
- [[Magic Missle]] many targets and always hits 
- [[Scorching Ray]] many targets 
	- works well with [[Empowered Spell]] (reroll up to 5 die)
- [[Witch Bolt]] huge continuous damage but needs [[Concentration]] so not compatible with [[Shadow Blade]]
## Support/Defense
- Heal with [[Healing Hands]]
- End [[Blinded]], [[Deafened]], [[Paralyzed]] or [[Poisoned]] conditions with [[Lesser Restoration]]
- Cancel an allies [[Disadvantage]] with [[Restore Balance]]
- [[Shield]] when you would be hit by a strong attack
