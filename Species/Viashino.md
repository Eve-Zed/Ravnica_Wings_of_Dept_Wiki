Viashinos are intelligent bipedal humanoid lizard-folk. They possess lean but muscular builds and can be agile, quick, and vicious when necessary. They generally come in shades of green, brown, and red, and prefer arid climates. Viashino have gray blood.

In Ravnican society, the viashino are members of the guilds, among them the [[Boros Legion]], [[Gruul Clans]], and [[Izzet League]].
