The Silhana are the Ravnican [[Elf|elves]] that are part of the [[Selesnya Conclave]]. The Silhana are master archers who stalk the rooftops of the city-plane. Some Silhana, the Elves of Deep Shadow, were cast out of the Conclave long ago and joined the Golgari.
