The [[Ledev Guardian|Ledev]] Wolf-riders of the [[Selesnya Conclave]] apprentice at a young age. Each rider raises a wolf pup from birth to serve as a mount, hoping that one day the two will share the honor of guarding the Great Concourse. The wolves grow up to be bear-sized.
