_Conjuration Cantrip_

**Casting Time**: Action
**Range**: Self (1,5m radius)
**Components**: V
**Duration**: Instantaneous

You create a momentary circle of spectral blades that sweep around you. All other creatures within 1,5m of you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 force damage.

This spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
