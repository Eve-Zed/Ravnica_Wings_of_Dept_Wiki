Azor aka Supreme Judge Azor I, First Judge Azorius, is a [[Sphinx]] [[Lawmage]] and [[Hieromancer]] and sees himself as the Arbiter of Law. He founded the [[Azorius Senate]] and wrote most of the original [[Guildpact]].
